# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

## Overview

This application is designed with privacy in mind.

The app processes notes and AI intent detection entirely on the user's device. No user data is uploaded, stored, or processed on external servers.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

Specifically:

- No account creation
- No cloud storage
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No telemetry
- No cookies
- No background data collection

All notes and AI-related processing remain local to the device.

## AI Processing

The application includes local AI-based intent detection functionality.

All AI processing happens offline on the user's device. No note content, prompts, or AI outputs are sent to external services or third parties.

## Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection for core functionality.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services for:
- analytics
- advertising
- remote AI processing
- cloud synchronization

## Data Security

Because data remains stored locally on the user's device, users are responsible for securing access to their device and backups.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the project maintainer through the GitHub repository.
